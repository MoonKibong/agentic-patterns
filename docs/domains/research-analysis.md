# Research And Analysis

Research and analysis workflows need context, source control, citation discipline, critique, and
human judgment over conclusions.

## Common Forces

- Agents can produce fluent but unsupported claims.
- Long research tasks need durable source lists and decision records.
- Tool outputs may be untrusted or stale.
- Humans need transparent provenance, not just polished summaries.
